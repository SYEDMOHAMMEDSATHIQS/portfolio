Automatic Shoe Polisher

First Prize Winner at Eareyes 2K25 and Ideathon'24

The Automatic Shoe Polisher is an innovative solution that automates the shoe polishing process. This project won first prize in both paper presentation and proof of concept categories at two separate events at Kongu Engineering College.

The system features sensors to detect shoe placement, automated brushing mechanisms, and polish application systems. It demonstrates my ability to integrate mechanical and electronic components into a functional prototype.
